About Roam & Board
Roam & Board helps you find free dispersed campsites on public land across all 50 states — National Forests, BLM land, and State Wildlife Areas. No reservations, no fees.
⚠️ Important Disclaimer
Campsite data is sourced from the USFS ArcGIS Recreation Sites database and curated field research. Conditions change. Roads wash out, fire restrictions come and go, and seasonal closures may not be reflected in real time.

Always verify before you go: call the local ranger district, check InciWeb.nwcg.gov for fire restrictions, and consult current Motor Vehicle Use Maps (MVUMs) for legal motorized access.

Roam & Board is not affiliated with the USFS, BLM, or any government agency. Use this tool as a starting point — not as authoritative guidance.
